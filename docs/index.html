How It Works

1

Fetch Riemann zeros

The non-trivial zeros of the Riemann zeta function, lying on the critical line Re(s) = 1/2.

2

Compute the explicit formula

Sum oscillatory terms from each zero to reconstruct the Chebyshev psi function — a prime counting signal.

3

Detect the spikes

Primes appear as jumps in the signal. Use statistical outlier detection to find them.

The Meaning

This demonstrates a deep duality in mathematics: the prime numbers and the Riemann zeta zeros contain the same information, viewed from different perspectives.

This connection is at the heart of the Riemann Hypothesis, one of the most famous unsolved problems in mathematics.
